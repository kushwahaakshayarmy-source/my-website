The
Way
I
Build.

01

Silence Before Structure

Every great building begins with a long listening. What does the site want? What does the light do at dawn? What does the air feel like at dusk? I listen before I draw.

02

Material as Memory

Stone remembers pressure. Water remembers gravity. I choose materials not for aesthetics alone, but for the memory they carry — the story embedded in their formation.

03

Space as Experience

Architecture is not backdrop — it is protagonist. A corridor that compresses then releases. A threshold that transforms. Every spatial sequence is choreography.
